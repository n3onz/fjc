First Jump Course

Intro and Safety

Intro

Welcome to your Category A/First Jump instruction! You are going to be taught how to skydive with instructors from our aircraft up to 14,500 ft and then deploy your own parachute. We will teach you how to deal with emergency situations. You will then take a written test. Some lessons are theoretical, some are practical and some are both. Don’t be afraid to ask questions, there is no such thing as a silly question!

Currency requirements – You need to stay as current as possible. Within 30 days is the USPA requirement otherwise you will need to attend further refresher training.

Once out of the aircraft you are on your own. Skydiving will always have inherent risks involved, if you can't accept them don't jump.
